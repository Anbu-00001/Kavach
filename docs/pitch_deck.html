AI voice cloning removed the last tell. Scammers impersonate police/CBI, keep an elderly victim on the line for hours, cut off their family, and drain them "to clear their name."

Why nothing helps the victimEvery tool protects the institution, not the person on the call

  • Carrier / spam filters block numbers — scammers rotate them in seconds.
  • Deepfake-voice detectors ask "is the voice fake?" — useless when a real human reads the script, and they break on phone audio.
  • All of them are cloud services — they'd have to stream a vulnerable person's private call to a server. Shady, and often illegal.
  • And they show a warning on a screen — to someone who often can't read it. Half of India's illiterate population is over 50; ~11% of rural elders are digitally literate.

The insightFight the part of the scam that never changes — the manipulation script

The voice keeps getting better. The playbook doesn't. Kavach detects the 8 tactics every scam uses:

UrgencySecrecyUntraceable payment Authority impersonationDistress hook IsolationOTP / credential probeRelationship spoof

A tiny int8 model scores the live transcript on-device, fuses the tactics into a risk level, and boosts dangerous combos (money + authority + isolation = the digital-arrest pattern).

What it doesFollows the call live and calls it

  • Listens on speaker, fully offline — SAFE…
  • …CAUTION as the pressure builds…
  • …"Likely a scam" — with the tactics in plain words and one action: hang up & call back.

Works two ways on the same engine: a live shield, and a background guardian with the screen off.

The breakthroughIt speaks up — for the person who can't read the screen

On HIGH, the phone says it out loud, in the user's own language, and buzzes:

"Stop. This may be a scam. Don't send money or share any code. Hang up and call your family."

Colour + voice + vibration. Pre-recorded & offline — needs no TTS voice on the phone, can't hallucinate. Validated on the OPPO A18.
